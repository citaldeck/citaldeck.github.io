How to Use Ideogram.ai

Create Your Image On Leonardo.ai

Type in what you need to create your building

Then copy your description into Ideogram.ai

Create Your Logo on Ideogram.ai

Paste your text into Ideogram's prompt box.

Add "Make me a logo about [insert your description] with the words [insert your logo text] on [select your color] background

Select 16:9 and hit "Generate"

Result Logo

The result logo you get will reflect the theme of your building.

Email Me Your Logo

Once you created your logo, you can email me your logo at psyblade@mail.com
