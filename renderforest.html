How to Use RenderForest

Remove the Background of Your Logo

Remove the background of the logo you created using ideogram.ai

Then save the transparent background logo to your PC.

Create Your Animated Logo With RenderForest

Register and Sign in and then click on the "Start Now" button.

Select Videos

Click on the Videos button

Select Intros and Logos

Scroll down and select Intros and Logos

Select See-All

Click on "See-all"

Select Template

Select the template of your choice and click the "Create Now" button.

Upload Your Transparent Logo

Upload your transparent logo onto your template and click the "Preview" button on the top blue bar.

Select Fast Preview

Select Fast Preview

Wait for Generation

Wait for it to generate...

Save Video Result

Finally save the video result by right-clicking on your mouse anywhere on the video and select "Save Video As"

Upload Video to YouTube

Once you uploaded your animated logo video onto YouTube, email me the link at psyblade@mail.com
