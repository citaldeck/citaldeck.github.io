Make Your Own Portfolio Page

Download the Portfolio Template

Download the Portfolio template here

Change the Template

Replace the bio with your own bio, and the picture with your own picture

Do the same for your project picture

Edit with Notepad++

Right-click on the index.html file and choose "Edit with Notepad++"

In Notepad++ scroll down till you see the contents of the bio

Change the template bio to your bio

Then save it.

Open index.html in the browser and you will see the changes.

Change the Picture

You can also change the profile picture to your own photo or an image you generated using Leonardo.ai or an image you got from the net.

Just replace the picture in the folder and make sure your image is named "pp.jpg"

(Optional) Change the Theme

You can also ask ChatGPT to change the color theme for you if you want.

Open your Portfolio webpage source code on your favorite editor...

... copy the CSS code between your <STYLE> tag and your </STYLE> tag...

... paste it into ChatGPT's prompt

... hit Shift-Enter to go to the next line

... Add in "change to a brown color palette theme"

... Copy the result given by ChatGPT

... Paste the result back to replace your webpage CSS code between your <STYLE> tag and your </STYLE> tag

... Save your webpage in your editor.

... See the changes in the browser!

Publish Your Portfolio to Github.io

Github.com

Register at Github.com and sign in.

Goto Your Profile Page

https://github.com/your_username

Or Goto the Home Page After You Register and Signed In

https://github.com

Create a New Repository Named [your_username].github.io

Add Files to Your Repository You Just Created

Drag Files From Your package-main Folder Into This Repository

Once the files have finished uploaded, click on the "Commit Changes" button

You Can Then See Your Portfolio Page Published at [your_username].github.io

Your webpage may take a few minutes to appear. Any changes you made to the webpage will also take a few minutes before you can see the changes.
